Is Cadence (AI DJ) totally free?

Yes. The Productivity Lounge and the Cadence Chatbot are entirely free to use. Cadence searches our curated archive of YouTube/YouTube Music playlists to match your focus state.

How accurate is the RT60 Calculator?

It uses Sabine's Formula, which provides an excellent approximation for rectangular rooms. While perfect accuracy requires impulse response testing with a measurement mic, our calculator will get you 90% of the way there for treatment planning.

Do I really need a hardware Audio Interface?

If you are using an XLR microphone, yes. If you are using a USB microphone, the interface (ADC and preamp) is built directly into the microphone itself.

Can I use the Web Audio Synth on mobile?

Yes, the Web Audio API is supported on all modern mobile browsers. You can generate Brown Noise or Rain directly on your phone while studying.
